NOVUM CAPITAL SHARES VENTURE CAPITAL INSIGHTS ON BLOCKCHAIN INVESTMENTS IN JAKARTA



Early this month, Novum Capital was at the Blockchain Asia Forum 2018: Blockchain Applications and Economics Indonesia Forum in Jakarta. Irwin Chee, Managing Consultant of Novum Capital, was the panelist on the main conference day.


During the panel discussion, Chee shared insights on venture capital valuation on blockchain investments with the forum’s 300 attendees which comprised of government officials, funds and blockchain projects. The number and variety of attendees reflect the growing interest in blockchain technology, as well as the cumulative efforts of crypto enthusiasts coming together to find ways in promoting mass adoption for blockchain technology.




HIGH-TECH CRYPTO EXCHANGE, COINJAX, RAISES SEED ROUND WITH NOVUM CAPITAL



Novum participates in CoinJax seed round and partners strategically with the exchange going forward. CoinJax is an international high-tech cryptocurrency exchange, built from ground up with the emphasis of scalability and security. CoinJax employs the latest proprietary security techniques and best industry practice. All funds are stored in cold wallets, with a multi-sig process to ensure strong corporate governance and compliance.




CLOUDMOOLAH HITS US$15 MILLION RAISE



Following a successful US$15 million Token Sale in April 2018, Cloud Alliance’s (commonly known as CloudMoolah) MOO Token (XMOO) has successfully listed on Coinsuper as its first exchange. Cloud Alliance’s flagship product, CloudMoolah, is a payment aggregator integrated within the Unity Technologies’ game development software and is offered alongside other in-app-purchase (IAP) products from technology giants such as Apple, Google, Amazon, Facebook, Xiaomi and Samsung. CloudMoolah allows Unity developers to enable in-app revenue collection from more than 500,000 retail point of sales across Southeast Asia where the average credit card penetration rate is less than 3%.




CONSENTIUM SUCCESSFULLY RAISES US$42 MILLION



Consentium sells out USD42M offering in token sales. Consentium allows peer-to-peer (P2P), multi-digital-currency transfers between users. The fintech platform also offers a transactional fee redistribution programme as an incentive to create and cultivate strong in-app communities. Consentium uses a reward system based on creation of quality community groups, comprising both amount of users and on-app reputation of users.




MARKET EXPANSION PROMPTS NOVUM CAPITAL TO SET UP SECOND OFFICE IN MALAYSIA



Novum Capital, one of Southeast Asia’s leading blockchain consultancy firms, announces its expansion into Malaysia. This expansion allows Novum Capital to better serve its Malaysia-based clientele. The move into Kuala Lumpur was prompted by its ongoing projects in the country. Currently, Novum Capital has two ongoing projects and expects to have a client base in Malaysia in the foreseeable future. This rising demand is a clear indication of companies wanting to fundraise through the blockchain.




NOVUM CAPITAL SUCCESSFULLY CLOSES MEGAX, A RETAIL BASED TOKEN GENERATION EVENT
