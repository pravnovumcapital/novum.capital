Problems with
Conventional
Solar Panels

1

High temperatures cause low efficiency


  • By using concentrated sunlight, it produces high temperature on the solar module.

  • Eg, LCPV: Temperature could reach as high as 150* celsius, which results in solar cell conversion efficiency to reduce tremendously. 1 degree raise in temperature on the solar cell reduces conversion efficiency by 0.5%.

  • Increased effectiveness but low efficiency.

2

Low efficiency results in long breakeven period



  • Solar panels have long breakeven period of 4 to 10 years, with efficiency in the range of 8 to 17 percent (comparatively low).

  • Solar panels have low reliability in harsh environment, hence shortening usable lifespan of solar module.

  • Less reliable solar module produces negative net clean energy, resulting in more harm than good for the environment.

High Efficiency and effectiveness patented solar solution.


Solar energy solution that achieves the high efficiency despite high temperatures.


  • Increases solar to electricity conversion efficiency by between 25% to 32%.

  • Enables a shorter investment breakeven period of 3 years.

  • Low Concentrated Photovoltaic (LCPV) concentrates sunlight at a lower concentration ratio of less than 100x using single or dual axis solar tracker with c-silicon or other solar cell.

  • Patented Solution: filed patent for Solarite – High efficiency Solar Module – Patent No. GB1708275.1, on 24th May 2017.

Solarite Blockchain:
Proof-of-Power (PoP)


Solarite blockchain employs the new Proof-of-Power (PoP) concept: Rewarding people who produce energy!


The first solar farm built by Solarite Technologies will be the basis for the Solarite Blockchain. Subsequent solar farms and installations built by Solarite Technologies will leverage on the blockchain for transaction verifications, which will result in a massive use case for its TAPP tokens. Private solar farms will be able to leverage on the Solarite blockchain to participate in its solar energy ecosystem.


This Proof-of-Power (PoP) concept uses less electricity to mine Solarite TAPP coins in comparison to mining bitcoins. The excess electricity generated by Solarite’s solar farm will be sold back to the grid and generate value for token holders.




TAPP MARKETPLACE


Solar farms built and managed by Solarite Technologies will be supplying electricity transmission directly or through a network of retailers in exchange for the receipt of TAPP coins. Facilitating trading liquidity on exchanges with the support of other cryptocurrencies.




TAPP COIN HOLDERS


Crypto-savvy consumers who stake their TAPP coins with Solarite




CONSUMERS


Day-to-day electricity consumers who wish to purchase the electricity generated by Solarite’s solar farms and attain cost savings in the electricity bills through its breakthrough solar energy solutions.




RESELLERS


Third party resellers of Solarite’s electricity supply who can bypass potential transmission hurdles between Solarite and end consumers due to geographical or regulatory factors. This includes private solar farm owners leveraging on the Solarite Proof-of-Power Blockchain.
